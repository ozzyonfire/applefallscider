Heritage

We couldn't think of a better way to celebrate Canada's sesquicentennial than to create a cider infused with real Maple Syrup!

Created using late-variety apples grown at Campbell's Orchards in Prince Edward County, this lightly carbonated cider has a refreshing balanced apple flavor that is sweetened using real local maple syrup. Full of body, this cider is a perfect blend of sweetness and acidity with an unmistakable quality that will delight your taste buds.

Heritage & Cherry

On a dark winter night, Colin Campbell was rummaging around in the cider house. In his clumsiness (he may have been sampling too much cider) he accidentally knocked a bucket of sour cherries into a batch of Heritage cider. The result was a delicious cider with a subtle hint of cherry tartness. Only limited quantities are available of this exclusive happy accident.
